About關於

Where music
meets movement

A pianist with over 11 years of teaching experience and a growing practice in ballet accompaniment. Trained from childhood in Taiwan's music gifted programme and graduated from Fu Jen Catholic University with a dual Bachelor's degree in Music (Piano Performance) and Art — with clarinet as a second instrument, her classical foundation spans solo, chamber, choral, vocal, instrumental, and jazz settings.

逾 11 年鋼琴教學資歷，同時深化芭蕾音樂合作的專業。自幼就讀台北市音樂資優班至台灣輔仁大學藝術與音樂雙學士畢，主修鋼琴、副修單簧管，古典訓練橫跨獨奏、室內樂、合唱、聲樂、器樂與爵士。

Performances span the National Concert Hall, Taipei City Council, Taipei City Government Theatre, the historic palazzos and churches of Noto Sicily, Arcadia Music Center in Los Angeles, and Národní divadlo in Prague.

演出場館涵蓋台灣國家音樂廳、台北市議會演藝廳、台北市政府親子劇場、義大利西西里各歷史宮殿與教堂、洛杉磯 Arcadia Music Center，以及布拉格國家劇院 Národní divadlo。

Currently accompanying classes at Gravity Ballet Taipei alongside Taiwan's leading dance educators, and deepening ballet music collaboration through the Formosa Ballet and Music Collaboration Association.

目前於 Gravity Ballet Taipei 擔任芭蕾課程鋼琴家，並持續於福爾摩沙芭蕾教育與音樂合作協會深化芭蕾音樂合作專業。

Piano Performance

Vocal Collaboration
Opera, choral, and jazz vocal accompaniment. Collaborative pianist and teaching assistant for the San Gabriel Valley Children's Chorus, Los Angeles, 2024.

Instrumental Collaboration
Chamber music and ensemble collaboration with strings, winds, and percussion. 2024: Arcadia Music Center cello ensemble, Los Angeles. 2018: Piano-marimba duo placed 2nd in Taipei City Music Competition. Clarinet: 2017 Ministry of National Defense wind orchestra; 2024 Fu Jen Jazz Orchestra soloist.
與弦樂、管樂及打擊樂器的室內樂與合奏合作。2024 年洛杉磯 Arcadia Music Center 大提琴合奏演出；2018 鋼琴合作曾獲台北市音樂比賽第二名。2017 年國防部示範樂隊管樂團聯合合奏演出、2024 年輔仁爵士樂團單簧管獨奏。

Teaching教學

Piano Lessons鋼琴 課程

Music is not only for exams. The goal is to give every student something that stays long after the lesson ends — the depth and a genuine joy and love of music.

音樂不只是為了考試。每堂課的目標，是讓學生帶走比樂譜更持久的東西——底蘊以及對音樂真正的享受與喜愛。

🎹
Personalised Approach
個人化教學
Curriculum shaped around each student's level, age, and goals — the lesson follows the learner, not a fixed syllabus.
依照每位學生的程度、年齡與目標量身設計，以學習者為核心。
